Language Learning app

People are learning new vocabulary every day. But learning a new language from scratch is hard and if you have a busy life, it’s hard to find time to learn. During the UX Fundamentals course of Careerfoundry, I designed a lesson based vocabulary learning app.

Used skills

User & Competitor research, personas, userflows, low-fidelity wireframing, paper-prototyping, usability testing

Hidden Gems

Hidden Gems is a mobile scavenger hunt app. The app is designed for people who want to go off the beaten path and (re)discover the city they are in together with friends and family. The functionalities will give the users a way to immerse themselves in their city by playing and creating scavenger hunts.

Used skills

user-centered design, competitive analysis, generative and evaluative research, business requirements, user personas, user journeys, mobile first, responsive design, information architecture, user flowss, iterative wireframing and prototyping, usability and preference testing, design documentation and presentation
